# Tapeline — fintwit YouTuber outreach research

Researched 2026-05-16. Five YouTubers identified in Chat 1's session as
potential targets for Tapeline outreach. Each entry below resolves the
"how do I contact this person" gap the earlier session noted but didn't
finish — most YouTubers don't expose email on their YouTube About tab,
so contact paths come from a mix of about-pages, LinkedIn bios, Patreon
hubs, and (in the worst case) X DMs.

**Pacing**: same rule as the podcast pitches — 1 per week, written from
scratch for that creator's audience, not template-pasted. Volume reads as
spam; specificity reads as a real founder pitch.

**Strategic note from Chat 1**: defer cold YouTuber outreach to Week 4
of the launch plan, after `/scorecard` has visible week-1+ traction. A
cold pitch from a zero-signal founder converts at 1-3%; the same pitch
with public-back-checked receipts converts 10-20%. Don't burn the highest-
leverage names early.

## What to NOT do

- Don't email all five at once. Even with different subject lines, send
  patterns get flagged by their inbox tools. One per week, spaced.
- Don't pitch Tapeline as "the new Finviz". The whole differentiator is
  the public scorecard + published formula — lead with that, not with
  feature parity to incumbents.
- Don't ask for a paid placement upfront. Editorial mentions convert
  better and cost nothing; paid integrations cost $1500-5000/mention
  with these creators and have lower halflife.
- Don't follow up before 14 days. Once is acknowledgement; twice is
  spam.
